How to use

Templates & Tools: Use the left sidebar to add text, shapes, or pick ready-made poster templates.

Drag & Resize: Click any item on the central canvas to drag it. Use the corner dots to scale or rotate it.

Edit Text: Double-click on any text box to rewrite its content natively on the canvas.

Properties: Select an item to change its color, font, or opacity using the right side panel.

Export: When done, click the PNG, JPG, or PDF buttons at the top right to download instantly.
